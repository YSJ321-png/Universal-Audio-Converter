📖 如何获取音频文件
How to Obtain Audio Files

1. 网易云官方客户端格式转换 / NetEase Cloud Music Official Conversion

Right-click a song in the NetEase client → "Tools" → "Format Conversion" (if available).

在网易云客户端右键歌曲 → “工具” → “格式转换”（部分版本支持）。

2. 浏览器开发者工具 (F12) / Browser DevTools (F12)

Play the song in the web player, press F12 → Network → filter "Media" or sort by size, find the .m4a/.mp3 file, right-click "Open in new tab", then Save.

播放网页版歌曲，按F12→Network→筛选“Media”或按大小排序，找到.m4a/.mp3文件，右键“在新标签页打开”后保存。

3. 录音法 (Audacity等) / Recording via Audacity

Use Audacity (free) or other recording software to capture system audio while playing the song, then export as WAV/MP3.

使用Audacity（免费）或其他录音软件，播放歌曲时录制系统声音，导出为WAV/MP3。

4. 第三方下载工具 / Third-party Downloaders

Tools like Listen1, YesPlayMusic, or MusicTools can aggregate and download songs from multiple platforms.

Listen1、YesPlayMusic、MusicTools等第三方工具可以聚合下载多个平台的歌曲。

💡 获得音频文件后，拖入转换器即可转成任意格式！
After obtaining the audio file, drag it into the converter to convert to any format!
